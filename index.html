        About RaiseKit
        
      
        RaiseKit helps student organizations and small nonprofits build practical fundraising plans fast. Instead of starting from scratch, users enter their org type, funding goal, and timeline to get a suggested strategy, outreach messaging, and a step-by-step execution plan.
        
      
        The long-term vision is to expand into grant matching, donor segmentation, and fundraising workflow automation — giving mission-driven teams the infrastructure that larger organizations already have.
        
      
        Built by Giancarla Burgos · Inspired by real experience in fundraising strategy, grant research, and donor communications.
