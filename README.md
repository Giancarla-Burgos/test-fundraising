# RaiseKit 🚀

**A lightweight fundraising copilot for student organizations and small nonprofits.**

> Generate a practical fundraising strategy, outreach templates, and a step-by-step action plan — in 30 seconds.

🔗 **[Live site →](https://giancarla-burgos.github.io/test-fundraising/)**

---

## The problem

Fundraising is time-consuming and hard to start. Student orgs and small nonprofits often lack the resources to hire consultants or build infrastructure — yet they're expected to raise real money to do real work.

## The solution

RaiseKit generates a practical fundraising playbook from just four inputs: org type, funding goal, time horizon, and priority. No backend, no account required. Just a usable plan in seconds.

## Why I built this

Built from experience working on fundraising strategy, grant research, and donor communications at EWB and ReadWorks. I wanted a tool I actually wished existed — something fast, opinionated, and designed for people who are learning as they go.

## MVP features

- **Strategy generator** — tailored recommendation based on org type, goal, and timeline
- **4-week action timeline** — week-by-week breakdown, adjusts to 2-week or 3-month horizons
- **Donor outreach email template** — fill-in-the-blank copy you can send today
- **Grant category starter list** — curated by org type and funding priority
- **Copyable checklist** — a concrete to-do list to get started immediately
- **"What to do today" box** — 5 concrete next steps at the top of every plan
- **Preset examples** — 3 one-click scenarios to demo the tool instantly
- **Copy buttons** — copy any section with one click
- **Download as .txt** — save your full plan as a text file, no backend needed
- **Confidence / fit badge** — tells you what type of campaign this plan is best for
